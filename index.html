INSTALLS

Whether indoors or outdoors, at home or in the workplace, we fulfill all of your installation needs: fixtures, electrical panels, electrical services, whole home surge protectors, wall packs, battery backups and more.

We provide power solutions for your kitchen, pools, spas, hot tubs, water heaters, furnaces, ovens, ranges, generators, pumps, ceiling fans, ventilation fans and all other electrical appliances and equipment. We will meet your electrical needs with ease.

PLANNING

We work with you and your team to plan the layout design, installations, custom lighting, and wiring required to bring your dreams to life. We can help you decide on placement, choice of fixtures and devices, interactivity, and the overall results you are looking for.

With our assistance, you can choose lighting options such as LED strip lighting, under-cabinet and closet accents, overhead countertop illumination, outdoor lighting, emergency lighting, pole lights, and shop lights with ease to bring everything together with beauty, functionality, and cohesion.

SERVICE

No service call is too large or small, we maintain it all. We will keep your systems in top shape for years to come. From straight-forward upkeep, upgrades and repairs, to complete electrical renovations and restorations, we will meet your needs with our skilled team of electricians.

We handle all general electrical maintenance, as well as lightning inspections, LED lighting retrofitting, smoke detector and carbon monoxide detector inspections and installations, voltage drop analysis and correction, and electrical troubleshooting for all other issues.
